Welekade Old Market

Old market

The old welekade Market is a historic colonial building in Badulla. It has been recognized as the commercial trade centre established by the British in 1889. Shoppers looking for some souvenirs during their Sri Lanka visit will not ignore the famous Old Welekade Market in Badulla. This market appeals to the visitors as they feel as if they are back to the past era.
